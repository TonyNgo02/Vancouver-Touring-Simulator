Sightseeing Tour

Time Duration: 4-5 hours

This escape from the city is an ideal way to experience the natural wonders surrounding Vancouver and Whistler. Gaze out over ever-changing landscapes surrounding our Sea to Sky Highway, with scenic stops at the 335 meter Shannon Falls and ride up the awe-inspiring Sea to Sky Gondola with views of spectacular Howe Sound. Continuing the journey to Whistler, this world famous ski resort also hosted many 2010 Olympic outdoor sporting events. Spend an afternoon exploring eclectic downtown shops and cafes, hiking along nature trails, or relaxing and breathing in the fresh mountain air.

Prices (per):

  • Adults (ages 16 - 63): $149.99 + tax
  • Seniors (ages 64+): $99.99 + tax
  • Children (ages 8 - 16): $84.99 + tax

Hiking Tour

Time Duration: 3-5 hours

Experience Vancouver's vibrant culture, scenery and famous landmarks on our hiking tour. Walk or ride along the brilliant Vancouver Seawall through Stanley Park, English Bay & Granville Island. Jump aboard a little ferry and sail through False Creek before biking our way through Olympic village, China Town and Gastown.

Prices (per):

  • Adults (ages 16 - 63): $69.99 + tax
  • Seniors (ages 64+): $54.99 + tax
  • Children (ages 8 - 16): $39.99 + tax

You may bring your own bike on our guided hike, or rent a bike for $14.99/person + tax

Sea / Boat Tour

Time Duration: 4 hours

Indulge in the magnificent views of Vancouver's coastline!

Your trip starts with a relaxing cruise along Vancouver’s Inner Harbour, which then soars up to 55 km/h as you cross through the Burrard Inlet. Along the way you’ll enjoy the sights of Vancouver’s Coal Harbour, the North Shore, English Bay, False Creek and Stanley park. Tours are 90 mins in length and depart hourly from Granville Island.

Prices (per):

  • Adults (ages 16 - 63): $59.99 + tax
  • Seniors (ages 64+): $44.99 + tax
  • Children (ages 8 - 16): $37.99 + tax
