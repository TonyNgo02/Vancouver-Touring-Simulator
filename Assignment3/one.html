ABOUT US

ABOUT VAN ADVENTURES

VAN Adventures is committed to providing its customer with an oustanding experience, no matter the type of tour. We are a new and fresh touring agency that is looking to spice up the industry! We offer several different and unique types of tours and are always open to customer opinions to create new ones as well! From sightseeing, to boat-riding, to street-food touring, we have it all. Be sure you to bring your camera because you won't want to forget these moments!

OUR STORY

People have many reasons to explore. They explore to learn; they explore to make new friends; they explore to enrich their connection to new places and new people; they explore to satisfy their curiosity; they explore to escape their routine; or they simply explore for fun and adventure.

By exploring we turn the unknown into the familiar, and strangers into friends.

We are explorers ourselves, constantly looking deeper for new stories to share and interesting places to experience. We are well aware of the special connection people have with our community, and being a host means we celebrate the West Coast with you—we offer options to guide you and inspire you in the way that suits your style.

ABOUT OUR STAFF

At VAN Adventures, your comfort and safety is our top priority! Your experience starts with our friendly and knowledgeable tour staff. They can assist you with information on the many restaurants, sights, and activities in the area. Interested in a tour? Simply talk to our staff and they can help you on to the next available departure. When you sign up for a tour at VAN Adventures, rest assured that you are in good hands.

All of our crew members are uniformed and extensively trained. Your crew will also provide a comprehensive and entertaining live narration explaining all of the unique and captivating features of your designated tour. See something on the tour that we didn’t mention? Just ask! Our tour is interactive and your crew is more than happy to answer any questions you may have or even take a picture of you at the helm! Our crew is extensively trained in customer service as well, so you are guaranteed to encounter the friendliest and most knowledgeable staff. In fact, if you don’t enjoy your experience with us simply let us know and we’ll do what it takes to make it right!
